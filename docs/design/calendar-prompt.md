# Even — shared calendar page prompt (paste into Claude Design)

Context: iPhone screen (402×874) for Even, the two-partner household app in
this project. New tab: the household's shared calendar. Style consistent with
the existing Even designs in this project.

Design a proper calendar view:
- Month grid (swipe/step between months), days marked when something is due —
  distinguish who owns each item (the two member colors) and multiple items
  on one day.
- Selecting a day shows its items below the grid: title, owner, amount if
  any, and whether it's on Google Calendar (link affordance).
- An agenda strip or list of the upcoming items for the current week under
  or beside the grid — whatever reads best.
- Empty states: a month with nothing due; a selected day with nothing.
- Item types to represent: bills to pay, appointments, renewals, and
  household tasks with due dates.
- Include a hint that the calendar is shared with Google ("Even" calendar
  both partners can subscribe to).
